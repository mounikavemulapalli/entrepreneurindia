Conference Schedule 2024

5 Incredible Stages

THEME: Roadmap to India 2030   As the world's biggest democracy - India has also shown the pathway of democratization of entrepreneurship to the world. With AI making waves - entrepreneurship and not employment is the future of growth. By 2030 every Indian has to dream of a startup and gather resources, network, know-how, and experience to get it right. Entrepreneur 2024 paves the way for Shining India by creating possibilities for every Indian looking to enhance their entrepreneurial and personal growth capabilities with insights on:

  • What are the sectors of the future?  
  • Where do investment trends point towards?  
  • Building Sustainable Businesses  
  • Life and Work in the Age of AI  
  • How startups and Corporations can find new synergies?
