About The Event

Entrepreneurs influence the future and use developing technologies to rethink our world as visionaries. The arrival of Web 3.0 brings a new level of excitement and uncertainty to the future. Entrepreneur 2024 gathers innovators - thinkers, disruptors, and change-makers - who provide us with a view into the future. Their ideas and viewpoints offer a glimpse into the future  that we eagerly await.
