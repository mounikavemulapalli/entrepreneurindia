About Entrepreneur 2024

Asia-Pacific is poised to become the next start-up and entrepreneurship hotspot, and Entrepreneur Media is assisting new generation entrepreneurs and C-suites in propelling their brands into the next stage of growth. Due to the introduction of new age technologies such as artificial intelligence, automation, and blockchain, we are experiencing a total change in the way businesses are done, and the a day event will cover issues that will determine the course of organizations and entrepreneurs in the following decade.

Entrepreneur Global Media is back with its 14th edition of Entrepreneur 2024. Entrepreneur 2024 will host over 2000 top Entrepreneurs at Bharat Mandapam on the 4th of September. Entrepreneur 2024 is the final destination for entrepreneurs, investors, disruptors and innovators where they discuss, debate and dissect what the future holds in a vibrant business atmosphere. Entrepreneur 2024 will bring together the Movers and Shakers of India and Asia Pacific. Watch how they unleash their magic.
