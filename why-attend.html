Why Attend Entrepreneur 2024?

Entrepreneur 2024 is Entrepreneur®'s premier event, bringing together prominent thought leaders, CEOs, Founders, Co-founders, and other valued guests to share their expertise and ideas with their peers

Here are your

REASONS TO ATTEND

  • Learn from industry experts

    Our conference programme will feature the latest insights from some of the industry’s sharpest thinkers. Keep up-to-date with fast-moving industry trends and hear from the innovators driving things forward in retail, ecommerce & payments.
    • 120+ Speakers
    • 8 Summits (Leadership , Omni-Channel, New Commerce & D2C)
    • 30+ Sessions

  • Networking Opportunities

    With thousand plus of retail, ecommerce & payments professionals in attendance, meeting the right people to help improve your business has never been easier.
    • 77% decision makers (C-Suite, EVP, SVP, MD)
    • 1000+ delegates
    • 30+ hours of networking

  • Global Marketplace

    All of the industry’s leading suppliers will showcase the latest tech and digital solutions for in-store, online, omnichannel retail and payment solutions, helping you design better experiences for customers, increase operational effectiveness and drive sales.
    • Meet CEO’s, MD’s, Operations Directors, Marketing Directors, Heads of eCommerce, Payments, Technology, IT, Loss Prevention, Customer Experience and many more senior decision makers from the leading retailers and brands
